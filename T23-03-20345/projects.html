Title: A Comparative Study of Business Performance Between Physical and Hybrid Student Entrepreneurs at UDOM

Description: This study compares how student businesses perform when run physically versus using both physical and online methods.

Findings: Hybrid businesses reach more customers and earn more, but need digital skills. Physical businesses are easier to manage but limited by location.

Tools Used: Google Forms, Excel
